## Process Notes

### Critical Success Factors
- **Speed to POC**: Minimize time from initial contact to POC execution
- **POC Quality**: Ensure POCs demonstrate clear business value
- **Smooth Handoff**: Seamless transition from sales to delivery
- **Predictable Delivery**: Consistent, repeatable implementation process
- **Fast Collections**: Minimize DSO through clear milestones and deliverables

### Risk Mitigation Points
- Technical qualification during discovery phase
- POC prerequisites validation before commitment
- Clear success criteria definition for POC
- Formal stage gate reviews at key transitions
- Regular customer alignment checkpoints

### Optimization Opportunities
- Standardized deliverable templates reduce creation time
- Reusable POC scenarios accelerate validation
- Defined RACI matrix prevents delays
- Automated invoicing triggers at milestones
- Continuous process improvement based on metrics
